About Kynza

A new way to work towards financial independence

Kynza was created as a means for us to support eachother in reaching our financial goals. Before banking institutions, communities relied on one another. ROSCAs provide individuals who might not have access to financial institutions an opportunity to save funds. Often, members of saving groups share familial, cultural or geographical aspects.

Within you Kynza community, members of your choosing will pool their money into a common fund, organized around montly contributions. A single member withdraws the lump sum at the beginning of the cycle, and the proccess repeats until everyone has benefited equally! Empowering people and keeping communities in control.
