About us

Facilitating (ROSCA) usage for the digital age, Kynza streamlines the entire rosca experience in one consolidated platform, with all the tools you need at your fingertips.

Kynza, looks to preserve ROSCA's time-tested peer-to-peer mutual aid model.

Kynza, provides you with the tools to harness the power of community with barrier-free setup, communication and accounting features. Simply invite and pool with your own trusted networks and we'll take care of the rest.
